About StreamFare

Welcome to StreamFare

StreamFare is a personal media streaming platform designed for movie enthusiasts who want complete control over their own digital library. Instead of relying on multiple streaming subscriptions or searching through physical hard drives, StreamFare brings your entire movie collection together into one modern, searchable, Netflix-inspired experience.

Whether you're browsing your favorite action films, discovering forgotten classics, or organizing thousands of titles, StreamFare makes your personal media library easy to explore and enjoy.

Our Mission

To give users a beautiful, fast, and secure way to organize, search, and stream their own movie collections from anywhere in the world.

We believe your media should belong to you—not disappear when a streaming service removes it from their catalog. StreamFare is built around ownership, organization, and accessibility.

Why StreamFare?

Many people have spent years building digital movie collections stored on external hard drives or network storage devices. Accessing those collections is often inconvenient, with limited search capabilities and outdated interfaces.

StreamFare transforms a personal movie archive into a polished streaming experience, making it easy to browse, search, organize, and stream your media from virtually anywhere while maintaining complete ownership of your collection. Personal media servers and cloud-hosted libraries have become increasingly popular ways for users to access their own content across multiple devices.
